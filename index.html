Dissertation: How guided skill-progression affects player experience VS unguided?

Description:Created a game in Unity to support the study of player experience by creating a simple skill-progression system with a guided aspect and unguided skill progression system.

Using Player Experience Inventory to measure the experience found from both systems.

Engine: "Unity Engine"

Written Tasks:

  • Paper Prototype Planning
  • Research into Skill Systems (Diablo 2)
  • Anonymised Questionnaire to find results for player experience
  • Group Reflection

Technical Tasks:

  • Taking advantage of Unity's Context Menu to test the Skill System
  • Testing if right amount of points allocated when enemy is killed.
  • Creating Simple and not Complex designs for the skill system to avoid overwhelming the player.
  • Ease of use when interacting with the skill systems.

Game Key Features:

  • Use of Popular Game Design choice to reflect acquired & unacquired skill (grey = not chosen)/ (green = skill chosen)
  • Ability Points
  • Boss Fight
  • Challenge depending on player choice of skills
  • Music & Audio
  • Easy Controls
  • Toggle - Guided or Unguided
